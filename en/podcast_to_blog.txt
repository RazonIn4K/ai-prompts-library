3. FIVE SOCIAL MEDIA POSTS:
   - Create 5 standalone posts suitable for LinkedIn, Twitter, or Instagram
   - Each post should be 1–2 sentences
   - Include at least one call-to-action or thought-provoking question
   - Vary the tone and focus across the posts
   - Make them shareable and engaging

FORMAT: Clearly label each section ("BLOG POST", "SHOW NOTES", "SOCIAL POSTS").
