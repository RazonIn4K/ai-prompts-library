# Premium Legal-Style Quiz Copy

## Purpose
Complete question text, helper microcopy, and CTA labels for a high-end legal services qualification quiz. Designed for attorney practices, law firms, or legal service providers seeking to pre-qualify prospective clients.

## Tone
Polished, authoritative, reassuring, and professional. Conveys expertise while remaining accessible.

---

## 1. Quiz Intro Screen

### Headline
**See If We're the Right Fit for Your Legal Matter**

### Subheadline
Answer a few brief questions so we can understand your situation and determine how we may be able to assist you.

### Helper Microcopy
> This confidential assessment takes approximately 2 minutes. Your responses help us provide tailored guidance and ensure we connect you with the appropriate legal expertise.

### CTA Button
**Begin Your Assessment**

### Trust Indicators (optional placement)
- "Responses protected by attorney-client privilege"
- "No obligation to proceed"
- "Typically responds within 1 business day"

---

## 2. Qualification Questions

### Question 1: Matter Type

**Question Text:**
What type of legal matter are you seeking assistance with?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> Select the category that best describes your situation. If you're unsure, choose the closest match.

**Options:**
- Business & Corporate
- Employment & Workplace
- Contracts & Agreements
- Litigation & Disputes
- Intellectual Property
- Real Estate & Property
- Estate Planning & Probate
- Other / I'm Not Certain

---

### Question 2: Timeline & Urgency

**Question Text:**
How would you describe the urgency of your legal matter?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> Understanding your timeline helps us prioritize appropriately and allocate the right resources.

**Options:**
- Immediate: I have a pressing deadline or urgent situation
- Near-term: I need to address this within the next 30 days
- Planning ahead: I'm being proactive about a future matter
- Exploratory: I'm gathering information at this stage

---

### Question 3: Prior Representation

**Question Text:**
Have you previously worked with legal counsel on this matter?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> This helps us understand where you are in the process and how we can add value.

**Options:**
- Yes, I currently have representation but am seeking a second opinion
- Yes, but that engagement has concluded
- No, this is a new matter
- I've consulted informally but have not retained counsel

---

### Question 4: Decision Authority

**Question Text:**
Are you the primary decision-maker regarding legal representation for this matter?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> Knowing who is involved in the decision helps us tailor our consultation.

**Options:**
- Yes, I make these decisions independently
- I'm the primary contact, but others will be involved in the final decision
- I'm researching on behalf of someone else
- This involves a business entity with multiple stakeholders

---

### Question 5: Engagement Readiness

**Question Text:**
If our practice is a good fit, how soon are you prepared to move forward?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> There's no commitment implied—we simply want to understand your readiness level.

**Options:**
- Ready to engage immediately upon finding the right counsel
- Within the next 1–2 weeks
- Within the next month
- Timeline is flexible; I'm evaluating options

---

### Question 6: Investment Capacity

**Question Text:**
What range have you budgeted for legal services related to this matter?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> This information is confidential and helps us recommend appropriate service options. Select the range that best reflects your expectations.

**Options:**
- Under $5,000
- $5,000 – $15,000
- $15,000 – $50,000
- $50,000+
- I need guidance on what to expect
- Prefer not to disclose at this time

---

### Question 7: Contact Preference

**Question Text:**
What is your preferred method of communication?

**Helper Microcopy:**
> We respect your time and will use your preferred channel for all correspondence.

**Options:**
- Phone call
- Video conference
- Email correspondence
- No preference

---

## 3. Path-Specific Messaging

### Path A: Qualified Prospect

**Headline:**
**Thank You—You Appear to Be an Excellent Fit**

**Body Copy:**
Based on your responses, your matter aligns well with our areas of practice and expertise. We would welcome the opportunity to discuss your situation in greater detail during a confidential consultation.

**What to Expect:**
- A senior member of our team will review your submission
- You'll receive a response within one business day
- Your initial consultation will focus on understanding your objectives and outlining potential strategies

**Helper Microcopy:**
> The next step is to select a time that works for your schedule. There is no obligation, and our conversation will remain confidential.

**Primary CTA:**
**Schedule Your Consultation**

**Secondary CTA (optional):**
Request a callback instead

---

### Path B: Soft Reject / Not Qualified

**Headline:**
**Thank You for Your Interest**

**Body Copy:**
We appreciate you taking the time to complete this assessment. Based on the information provided, your matter may fall outside our current areas of focus, or the timing may not align with our availability.

However, we don't want to leave you without guidance.

**What We Recommend:**
- Review our resource library for general information on your matter type
- Consider reaching out to [Local Bar Association / Referral Service] for a practitioner better suited to your needs
- If your circumstances change, we encourage you to revisit this assessment

**Helper Microcopy:**
> We believe in connecting individuals with the right counsel, even when that counsel isn't us. The resources below may help you take the next step.

**Primary CTA:**
**Browse Our Resource Library**

**Secondary CTA:**
Contact Us With Questions

---

### Path C: Needs More Information

**Headline:**
**We'd Like to Learn More**

**Body Copy:**
Thank you for your responses. Your situation may benefit from a brief preliminary conversation before we can determine the best path forward. This is not uncommon for matters that involve multiple considerations or unique circumstances.

**Helper Microcopy:**
> A short call with our intake coordinator will help clarify the details. This call is complimentary and carries no obligation.

**Primary CTA:**
**Request a Preliminary Call**

**Secondary CTA:**
Provide Additional Details via Email

---

## Usage Notes

- Adjust qualification thresholds based on practice area and capacity
- Budget question can be removed for practices with fixed-fee structures
- Soft reject messaging should always provide value through referrals or resources
- Confirmation copy should reinforce professionalism and reduce no-shows
