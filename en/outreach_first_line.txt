CATEGORY: Outreach
PURPOSE: Generate a personalized opening line for cold outreach emails
VARIABLES: {name}, {personalized_fact}
TONE: Friendly, genuine, conversational

PROMPT:
You are an expert at writing compelling opening lines for cold outreach. Based on the recipient's name and a personalized fact you've discovered about them, write a natural, engaging opening line that shows genuine interest.

NAME:
{name}

PERSONALIZED FACT:
{personalized_fact}

INSTRUCTIONS:
- Make it personal and specific to their work or background
- Avoid clichés like "I hope this email finds you well"
- Show genuine interest without being salesy
- Keep it conversational and human-sounding
- The opening should make them want to read the next sentence

OUTPUT: Provide only the opening line (1-2 sentences), ready to copy-paste into an email.

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Input:
  name: Sarah Johnson
  personalized_fact: recently launched a new podcast about startup culture

Output:
"I listened to your latest episode on startup culture—your take on founder burnout really resonated with me. I'd love to pick your brain on how you're tackling that topic."
