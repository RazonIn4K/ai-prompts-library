# Upwork Funnel Job Prompt Pack

This pack contains a set of prompts designed to help you generate high-quality Upwork proposals and project plans using your IDE AI (Cursor, Jules, Stitch, etc.).

## How to Use

1.  **Open your IDE AI chat.**
2.  **Copy & Paste the Universal Header:**
    - Open `00_UNIVERSAL_HEADER.md` and copy the entire content.
    - Paste it into the chat.
3.  **Copy & Paste the Job-Specific Add-On:**
    - Choose the add-on file that matches your current job (e.g., `01_JOB_A_URGENT_SYSTEME.md`).
    - Copy the content and paste it into the chat _immediately after_ the header (in the same message or the next one, depending on context window).
4.  **Paste the Upwork Job Description:**
    - Copy the full text of the Upwork job posting.
    - Paste it at the very end.
5.  **Run the Prompt:**
    - Hit enter and let the AI generate your Requirements Snapshot, Milestone Plan, Implementation Checklist, Project Scaffold, Proposal Draft, and Risks & Questions.

- `00_UNIVERSAL_HEADER.md`: The core instructions and persona. **Use this for every job.**
- `01_JOB_A_URGENT_SYSTEME.md`: Urgent Systeme.io Funnel Setup.
- `02_JOB_B_SYSTEME_TEMPLATE.md`: Systeme.io Template & Digital Products.
- `03_JOB_C_CRO_LANDING_PAGE.md`: CRO / Landing Page (Organic Dropshipping).
- `04_JOB_D_CLICKFUNNELS_REVAMP.md`: Revamp Gutter Company Website (ClickFunnels).
- `05_JOB_E_BREAKDANCE_WORDPRESS.md`: Funnel & Landing Page Builder (Breakdance/WordPress).
- `06_JOB_F_GUMROAD_EBOOK.md`: Paid Media & Funnel Specialist (Gumroad eBook).
- `07_JOB_G_FLODESK_STRATEGY.md`: Marketing & Funnel Strategist (Beauty Salon).
- `08_JOB_H_QUICKSEW_UX_CRO.md`: UX/UI + CRO Expert (Quicksew).
- `09_JOB_I_INFO_PRODUCT_GROWTH.md`: Growth Marketer (Info Product).
