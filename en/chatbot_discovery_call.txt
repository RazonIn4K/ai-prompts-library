CATEGORY: Discovery & Chatbot Strategy
PURPOSE: Lead a chatbot discovery call that clarifies goals, conversational scope, training data, integrations, and success metrics
VARIABLES: {client_overview}, {chatbot_goal}, {existing_content}, {languages_needed}, {integration_targets}, {call_duration}
TONE: Curious, methodical, user-centric, plain-spoken

PROMPT:
You are preparing to guide a chatbot discovery call focused on AI assistants for support, lead capture, or internal enablement. Leverage the context below to shape a clear agenda, question set, and follow-up plan.

CLIENT OVERVIEW:
{client_overview}

PRIMARY CHATBOT GOAL:
{chatbot_goal}

EXISTING CONTENT / KNOWLEDGE SOURCES:
{existing_content}

LANGUAGES REQUIRED:
{languages_needed}

INTEGRATIONS TO CONSIDER:
{integration_targets}

CALL DURATION:
{call_duration}

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Outline an agenda covering kickoff, user/intent mapping, data readiness, integration feasibility, and adoption/analytics.
2. Provide probing questions for each agenda block, including when to dig deeper (e.g., compliance, multilingual handoffs, handover to humans).
3. Recommend checkpoints for validating training data quality (docs, knowledge bases, transcripts) plus gaps to flag.
4. Suggest at least three success metrics tied to the stated goal (containment rate, lead capture rate, SLA reduction, etc.).
5. Finish with a recap script that confirms deliverables, timeline estimate, and a CTA for a prototype or paid discovery sprint.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Agenda with bullet descriptions
- Question bank grouped by agenda block
- Data & integration validation checklist
- Suggested success metrics list
- Closing recap script

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Replace the variables with your research notes, paste into your AI model, and you will receive a structured call guide you can keep on screen while steering a chatbot discovery session.
