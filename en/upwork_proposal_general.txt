CATEGORY: Proposals & Sales
PURPOSE: Draft compelling Upwork proposals from client job posts and your capabilities
VARIABLES: {client_job_post}, {my_capabilities}
TONE: Professional, empathetic, confident, action-oriented

PROMPT:
You are an expert Upwork proposal writer. Based on the client's job post and your capabilities, draft a compelling, personalized proposal that stands out and addresses their specific needs.

CLIENT JOB POST:
{client_job_post}

MY CAPABILITIES:
{my_capabilities}

REQUIREMENTS:
- Open with empathy: Show you genuinely understand their challenge
- Outline 3-5 clear, numbered steps in your proposed approach
- Highlight how your specific capabilities directly address their stated needs
- Include a strong call-to-action (CTA) at the end
- Keep it under 300 words—concise and impactful
- Use a professional but friendly tone (avoid robotic language)
- Avoid generic template language—make it specific to this client

OUTPUT: Provide ONLY the proposal text, formatted and ready to copy-paste directly into Upwork. No additional commentary.

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Copy the prompt, replace {client_job_post} with the Upwork job description and {my_capabilities} with your relevant skills and experience. The output will be a personalized, ready-to-submit proposal that demonstrates empathy and strategic thinking.
