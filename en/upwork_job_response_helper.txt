CATEGORY: Proposals & Sales
PURPOSE: Turn an Upwork job post plus your capabilities into a 200–300 word response that mirrors the client goal, outlines deliverables, and ends with a clear CTA
VARIABLES: {client_job_post}, {my_capabilities}, {cta_offer}
TONE: Confident, specific, collaborative, no hype

PROMPT:
You are a proposal specialist helping me answer an Upwork job quickly without sounding generic. Use the context below to craft the full response.

CLIENT JOB POST:
{client_job_post}

MY CAPABILITIES + PROOF:
{my_capabilities}

PREFERRED CALL TO ACTION:
{cta_offer}

REQUIREMENTS:
- Length: 200–300 words max.
- Opening: 1–2 sentences mirroring the client's stated objective and constraints.
- Delivery section: 3–5 bullet points highlighting what I will deliver, each starting with a verb and tying back to their pain point.
- Proof: weave in specific tools, metrics, or past outcomes pulled from my capabilities section.
- CTA: close with a concrete next step referencing {cta_offer} (e.g., "Let's review your Zapier map tomorrow, 15 minutes").
- Style: practical language with no fluff; assume the reader skims.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
[Greeting + Mirrored Goal]

[Value Paragraph]

- Bullet deliverable 1
- Bullet deliverable 2
- Bullet deliverable 3 (add more up to 5 as needed)

[CTA paragraph referencing {cta_offer}]

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Paste this entire prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, replace the placeholders with the job description, your relevant proof, and the desired call to action. The output will be a copy-ready Upwork response that balances empathy, proof, and a clear next step.
