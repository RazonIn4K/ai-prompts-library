# 01: Executive GTM / Investor Pitch

Paste this under the Universal Header:

```text
SCENARIO: Executive GTM / investor pitch deck for a SaaS/AI product. Audience = execs/investors. Goal = secure approval/funding + next meeting.

REQUIRED SECTIONS (target 12–15 slides):
- Cold open hook: market shift or urgent pain.
- Problem + stakes: quantify pain with 2–3 data points.
- ICP + use cases: who buys, who benefits, fastest land-and-expand path.
- Product snapshot: 3 hero capabilities + architecture at a glance.
- Differentiation: 3 crisp edges vs. status quo/competitors.
- Traction/proof: logos or anonymized wins, core metrics, demo GIF callouts.
- GTM plan: channels, offers, motions (PLG, outbound, partner), first 90-day plays.
- Pricing/packaging: simple grid with rationale.
- Roadmap: next 2–3 milestones tied to revenue or retention.
- Risks/mitigations: top 3 with owner.
- Ask/CTA: funding/approval + what happens in the next 7 days.

RULES:
- Keep titles action-oriented (≤5 words).
- Every proof slide must include metric + source + short note for presenter.
- Visual cues: market map, simple architecture, traction bar chart, GTM swimlanes, roadmap timeline.
```
