# 04: Workshop / Training Playbook

Paste this under the Universal Header:

```text
SCENARIO: 60–90 minute workshop/training deck. Audience = practitioners/leads. Goal = teach, practice, and leave with a checklist.

REQUIRED SECTIONS (12–16 slides):
- Title + promise: what attendees will leave with.
- Agenda with timings: modules + breaks.
- Baseline: quick poll or self-assessment (3 questions).
- Framework: the core model in 3–5 steps.
- Deep dive per step: micro-instructions + pitfalls.
- Live exercise(s): prompt, materials, expected output, timing.
- Examples/teardowns: 2–3 good vs. bad patterns.
- Tools/links: docs, templates, repos, dashboards.
- QA/tests: how to validate the work (checklist).
- Action plan: 7-day and 30-day next steps.
- CTA: book follow-up, office hours, or implementation sprint.

RULES:
- Include “Facilitator notes” on timing and what to watch for during exercises.
- Visual cues: framework diagram, timer icon for exercises, checklist cards.
- Keep copy instructional; avoid marketing fluff.
```
