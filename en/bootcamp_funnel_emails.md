## Implementation Notes

### Recommended Email Timing

**Nurture Sequence:**
- Email 1: Immediately after opt-in
- Email 2: Day 2
- Email 3: Day 4
- Email 4: Day 6
- Email 5: Day 7 (or 24 hours before enrollment close)

**Webinar Reminders:**
- Confirmation: Immediately after registration
- 24-hour reminder: Day before webinar
- 1-hour reminder: 60 minutes before
- Starting now: At webinar start time
- No-show: 2 hours after webinar ends
- Replay expiring: 24 hours before replay removal

**Cart Abandonment:**
- First nudge: 1 hour after abandonment
- Second nudge: 24 hours after abandonment
- Price warning: 24 hours before deadline
- Final hours: 3-6 hours before deadline

**Onboarding:**
- Welcome: Immediately after purchase
- Community intro: Day 1
- Expectations: Day 3
- Call reminder: Day before first call
- Check-in: Day 7

### Segmentation Tags to Create

- `bootcamp_lead` ‚Äî Opted in, not purchased
- `bootcamp_buyer` ‚Äî Purchased, active member
- `webinar_registered` ‚Äî Signed up for webinar
- `webinar_attended` ‚Äî Showed up live
- `webinar_no_show` ‚Äî Registered but didn't attend
- `cart_abandoner` ‚Äî Started checkout, didn't complete
- `hot_lead` ‚Äî High engagement, hasn't purchased
- `onboarding_week_1` ‚Äî First week of program
- `engaged_student` ‚Äî Active in community/calls
- `at_risk` ‚Äî Low engagement, needs outreach
