CATEGORY: Discovery
PURPOSE: Comprehensive questions to scope chatbot projects (purpose, audience, functionality, integrations, timeline)
VARIABLES: None (reference guide)
TONE: Consultative, thorough, technical and business-focused

PROMPT:
Use these discovery questions to fully understand a prospect's chatbot project requirements before proposing a solution. Cover all key dimensions: purpose, audience, functionality, languages, resources, integrations, and budget.

CHATBOT PURPOSE & GOALS:
1. What is the primary goal of your chatbot? (customer support, lead generation, FAQ automation, sales assistance, etc.)
2. What specific problems are you trying to solve with a chatbot?
3. How do you measure success? (response time, user satisfaction, cost savings, conversion rate, etc.)

AUDIENCE & USER BASE:
4. Who will be using this chatbot? (customers, employees, leads, specific demographic?)
5. What is your expected monthly/yearly usage volume?
6. What devices/platforms will users access it from? (website, Facebook, WhatsApp, Slack, email?)
7. What is the technical comfort level of your typical user?

SCOPE & FUNCTIONALITY:
8. What are the top 10 questions or topics the chatbot should handle?
9. Are there scenarios where the chatbot should hand off to a human? When and how?
10. Should it integrate with your existing systems? (CRM, help desk, email, knowledge base, payment systems?)
11. Do you want the chatbot to perform transactions (bookings, payments, subscriptions)?

LANGUAGES & LOCALIZATION:
12. What languages do you need the chatbot to support?
13. Do you have regional variations or local knowledge that affects responses?

EXISTING RESOURCES & DOCUMENTATION:
14. Do you have a knowledge base, FAQ section, or documentation we can use to train the chatbot?
15. How often do your FAQs or answers change? How will you keep the chatbot updated?
16. Do you have call logs, chat transcripts, or customer service data we can learn from?

INTEGRATIONS & TECHNICAL:
17. What systems does the chatbot need to connect to? (databases, APIs, third-party tools)
18. Are there compliance or data privacy requirements? (GDPR, SOC2, PCI-DSS, etc.)
19. What is your technical infrastructure like? (cloud, on-premise, managed?)

TIMELINE & BUDGET:
20. When do you want the chatbot live?
21. What is your budget for this project?
22. Do you need ongoing training, updates, and maintenance? What should that look like?

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Walk through these questions during your initial discovery meeting or send as a detailed questionnaire. The answers will give you everything needed to propose a tailored chatbot solution that matches their business needs, budget, and technical environment.
