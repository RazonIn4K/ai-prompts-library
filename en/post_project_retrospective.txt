CATEGORY: Post-Engagement
PURPOSE: Turn project delivery notes into a polished, client-facing recap for case studies or testimonials
VARIABLES: {client_name}, {client_company}, {project_name}, {engagement_scope}, {before_state}, {after_state}, {measured_results}, {client_quote}, {next_recommendation}
TONE: Appreciative, confident, partnership-focused, concrete

PROMPT:
You are wrapping up an automation/chatbot engagement and want to deliver a concise retrospective the client can share internally or externally. Use the context below to highlight outcomes without hype.

CLIENT NAME:
{client_name}

CLIENT COMPANY:
{client_company}

PROJECT NAME:
{project_name}

ENGAGEMENT SCOPE:
{engagement_scope}

BEFORE STATE / CHALLENGE:
{before_state}

AFTER STATE / SOLUTION:
{after_state}

MEASURED RESULTS / METRICS:
{measured_results}

CLIENT QUOTE OR FEEDBACK:
{client_quote}

NEXT RECOMMENDATION OR EXTENSION IDEA:
{next_recommendation}

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Draft a 2–3 sentence overview summarizing the client goal and the transformation achieved.
2. Create a "Highlights" section with 3 bullet points covering efficiency wins, revenue/CSAT impact, and tooling improvements, referencing specific metrics from {measured_results}.
3. Add a "Client Voice" paragraph that paraphrases or directly uses {client_quote} (attribute it to {client_name} / {client_company}).
4. Close with a "What’s Next" paragraph that references {next_recommendation} and invites a follow-up conversation.
5. Keep the recap under 250 words, ready to paste into a case study or testimonial request.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
[Overview paragraph]

**Highlights**
- Bullet 1
- Bullet 2
- Bullet 3

**Client Voice**
[Quote or paraphrased endorsement]

**What’s Next**
[Forward-looking paragraph]

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Paste this prompt into ChatGPT or Claude, replace the placeholders with your engagement details, and you will receive a polished retrospective you can email to the client or adapt into a case study slide.
