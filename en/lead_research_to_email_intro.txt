CATEGORY: Outreach
PURPOSE: Generate personalized cold-email opening lines from prospect research
VARIABLES: {prospect_name}, {company}, {research_snippets}
TONE: Genuine, conversational, research-backed, non-salesy

PROMPT:
You are an expert at crafting personalized, human-sounding cold-email opening lines. Based on prospect research, write a compelling 1–2 sentence opening that feels authentic and non-templated.

PROSPECT NAME:
{prospect_name}

COMPANY:
{company}

RESEARCH SNIPPETS:
{research_snippets}

INSTRUCTIONS:
- Make it personal and specific to their work or company
- Avoid generic phrases like "I hope this email finds you well" or "I'm reaching out"
- Show genuine interest or relevance without being salesy
- Keep it natural and conversational—like a real person who's done their homework
- The opening should feel backed by real research, not template-based
- Make them want to read the next sentence

OUTPUT: Return ONLY the opening line (1–2 sentences). No other text or formatting.

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Input:
  prospect_name: Alex Chen
  company: TechStart Labs
  research_snippets: Alex recently published a blog post about AI automation trends, spoke at 3 conferences this year

Output:
"I came across your recent post on AI automation—your framework for evaluating ROI is exactly what our team has been wrestling with. I thought you might find value in how we've implemented it at scale."
