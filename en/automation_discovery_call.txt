CATEGORY: Discovery & Strategy
PURPOSE: Prepare a structured automation discovery call agenda that uncovers bottlenecks, data sources, and success criteria
VARIABLES: {client_overview}, {current_stack}, {call_duration}, {my_capabilities}
TONE: Consultative, calm, practical, business-focused

PROMPT:
You are an automation consultant preparing for a live discovery call. Use the context below to design a step-by-step call plan that keeps the conversation grounded in business outcomes and technical feasibility.

CLIENT OVERVIEW:
{client_overview}

CURRENT STACK / WORKFLOWS:
{current_stack}

CALL DURATION:
{call_duration}

MY CAPABILITIES:
{my_capabilities}

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Provide a 3-phase call agenda (rapport & context, diagnostic deep dive, success alignment + next steps) with time boxes.
2. Within the diagnostic phase, list targeted questions grouped by process visibility, tool connectivity, and measurable impact. Each question should include a short note on why it matters.
3. Call out the data or artifacts I should request (dashboards, SOPs, exports) to validate assumptions after the call.
4. Flag likely automation candidates (e.g., scraping handoffs, CRM updates, report generation) based on the stack provided.
5. End with a closing script that recaps promised follow-ups and sets a concrete next step.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Agenda with timestamps
- Question list with rationale
- Requested artifacts
- Automation candidates table (Process | Pain Point | Proposed Automation)
- Closing script paragraph

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Paste this prompt into ChatGPT or Claude, replace the variables with the client info gathered from research or intake forms, and you will receive a ready-to-follow discovery call plan tailored to the automation stack in question.
