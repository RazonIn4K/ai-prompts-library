CATEGORY: Proposals & Discovery
PURPOSE: Evaluate client fit from job posts or discovery notes, highlighting risks and recommending a go/no-go decision
VARIABLES: {client_name}, {project_summary}, {job_post}, {discovery_notes}, {budget_signal}, {timeline_signal}, {working_style_signal}, {gut_check_notes}
TONE: Candid, analytical, risk-aware, respectful

PROMPT:
You are an engagement lead deciding whether to pursue a new automation/chatbot opportunity. Analyze the provided context and deliver a clear recommendation.

CLIENT NAME:
{client_name}

PROJECT SUMMARY:
{project_summary}

JOB POST (if available):
{job_post}

DISCOVERY NOTES (if available):
{discovery_notes}

BUDGET SIGNAL:
{budget_signal}

TIMELINE SIGNAL:
{timeline_signal}

WORKING STYLE SIGNAL (async, meetings, messiness):
{working_style_signal}

GUT CHECK NOTES:
{gut_check_notes}

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Create a "Fit Snapshot" table with columns Dimension | Evidence | Score (1-5). Dimensions: Problem Clarity, Budget Reality, Timeline Feasibility, Collaboration Style, Strategic Value.
2. Produce a "Risk & Red Flags" list highlighting potential issues. For each, include severity (Low/Med/High) and a mitigation idea.
3. State a "Decision" block that includes: Fit Score average, Recommended Action (Yes / No / Conditional), and a one-sentence justification referencing the key signal.
4. Add "If Yes" next steps (top 2 actions) or "If No" polite decline language. If recommendation is Conditional, list what must be confirmed.
5. Keep the entire output under 300 words while remaining specific.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Fit Snapshot table
- Risk & Red Flags list
- Decision block
- Next Steps paragraph(s)

EXAMPLE USAGE:
Paste in a job description or call notes plus your quick signals. The AI will return a concise go/no-go analysis you can use before sending a proposal or booking a follow-up.
